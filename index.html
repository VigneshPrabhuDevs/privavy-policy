Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

Welcome to Briefly. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a high-quality, AI-powered news summary experience.

1. On-Device AI & Your Data

Briefly is built with a "Reader First" philosophy. Unlike traditional cloud-based AI apps:

  • Local Processing: All news summarization is performed locally on your device using the Gemma 2B model.
  • No Article Tracking: The content of the articles you summarize never leaves your device and is not sent to our servers.

2. Information We Collect

To improve Briefly, we use Firebase Analytics to collect limited, non-identifying technical data:

  • App Performance: Crash reports and system stability data.
  • Usage Statistics: High-level events like "summary_generated" to understand which features are helpful.
  • Device Information: Device type and OS version.
